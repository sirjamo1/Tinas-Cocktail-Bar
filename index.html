Champagne Cocktail

Ingredients
  • 1 x Cointreau / Brandy / Galliano
  • 1 x Orange juice
  • Champagne
  • Angostura bitters
Instructions
  1. Pour orange liqueur in shallow bowl.
  2. Dip rim into bowl then rim with sugar.
  3. Add remaining orange liqueur and orange juice to glass.
  4. Add a dash of Angostura.
  5. Top up with Champagne.
X
Champagne Cocktail

This champagne cocktail variation is a refreshing and citrusy drink that is perfect for special occasions or celebrations. It is made by combining orange liqueur, orange juice, and a dash of angostura bitters in a cocktail shaker with ice. The mixture is then strained into a chilled champagne flute and topped with chilled champagne. The result is a delicious and effervescent drink with a sweet and tangy citrus flavor, balanced by a subtle spice and depth of flavor from the bitters. The bubbles from the champagne create a delightful effervescence, making it a perfect drink for toasting or sipping slowly. This champagne cocktail variation is a sophisticated and enjoyable twist on the classic recipe that is sure to impress your guests.

Brandy Alexander

Ingredients
  • 1 x Brandy
  • 1 x Creme de cacao
  • 1 x Thin cream
  • Ground nutmeg
Instructions
  1. Place brandy, creme de cacao and cream into cocktail shaker.
  2. Add crushed ice and shake.
  3. Strain through sieve into glass.
  4. Sprinkle with nutmeg.

Brandy Alexander

Cosmopolitan

Cosmopolitan
  • 2 x Citron vodka
  • 1 x Cointreau
  • 1 x Cranberry juice
  • 1 x Lime juice
  • Lime wedge
Instructions
  1. Chill glass.
  2. Add all Ingredients to shaker.
  3. Add ice and shake.
  4. Serve with lime wedge.
X
Ingredients

The Cosmopolitan, also known as the "Cosmo," is a popular and stylish cocktail that gained fame in the 1990s thanks to the TV show "Sex and the City." It is made by combining vodka, triple sec, cranberry juice, and lime juice in a cocktail shaker with ice. The mixture is then strained into a chilled martini glass and garnished with a twist of lime. The result is a bright and tangy drink with a perfect balance of sweetness and tartness. The vodka provides a smooth and clean taste, while the triple sec and cranberry juice add depth and complexity to the drink. The lime juice adds a refreshing acidity that complements the sweetness of the other ingredients. The Cosmopolitan is a sophisticated and refreshing cocktail that is perfect for a night out or as a signature drink at a party.

Spiced Ginger Mojito

Ingredients
  • 2 x Rum
  • 1/2 x Simple syrup
  • Splash of Ginger ale
  • 6 x Mint leaves
Instructions
  1. Muddle mint, simple syrup and 1/4 lime in cocktail shaker.
  2. Add spiced rum.
  3. Add ice and shake.
  4. Strain into glass filled with ice.
  5. Top with ginger ale.
  6. Garnish with lime and mint leaves.
X
Spiced Ginger Mojito

The Spiced Ginger Mojito made with 2 parts rum, 1/2 part simple syrup, a splash of ginger ale, and 6 mint leaves is a refreshing and flavorful cocktail that is perfect for a warm summer day. To make this cocktail, muddle the mint leaves in a tall glass with the simple syrup. Add ice to the glass, then pour in the rum and stir to combine. Top the drink with a splash of ginger ale and give it a quick stir. The result is a sweet and tangy drink with a hint of spice from the ginger ale. The rum adds a warm and mellow flavor, while the mint leaves provide a bright and fresh taste. The simple syrup balances the flavors and adds a sweetness that makes the drink perfect for sipping on a hot day. The Spiced Ginger Mojito made with these ingredients is a simple and delicious cocktail that is sure to be a hit with your guests.

Tequila Sunrise

Tropical Mocktail

Ingredients
  • 1/2 x Lemon
  • 1/2 x Lime
  • Orange juice
  • 1/4 x Grenadine
Instructions
  1. Add ice to glass.
  2. Add lemon and lime.
  3. Top up with orange juice.
  4. Add grenadine down side of glass with the back of a spoon.
X
Tropical Mocktail

The Tropical Mocktail is a simple and refreshing drink that is perfect for a brunch or a light refreshment. To make this mocktail, combine 1/4 oz of fresh lemon juice and 1/4 oz of fresh lime juice in a cocktail shaker with ice. Add a splash of orange juice and a dash of grenadine, then shake well until chilled. Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice and garnish with a slice of orange or a sprig of mint. The result is a tangy and sweet mocktail with a refreshing citrus flavor. The lemon and lime juices provide a tartness that is balanced by the sweetness of the orange juice and grenadine. The dash of grenadine adds a subtle sweetness and a deep red hue to the drink. The combination of these ingredients makes for a light and refreshing mocktail that is perfect for sipping on a warm day.
