Champagne Cocktail

Ingredients

  1. 1 x Cointreau / Brandy / Galliano
  2. 1 x Orange juice
  3. Champagne
  4. Angostura bitters

Instructions

  1. Pour orange liqueur in shallow bowl.
  2. Dip rim into bowl then rim with sugar.
  3. Add remaining orange liqueur and orange juice to glass.
  4. Add a dash of Angostura.
  5. Top up with Champagne.

Brandy Alexander

Ingredients

  1. 1 x Brandy
  2. 1 x Creme de cacao
  3. 1 x Thin cream
  4. Ground nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Place brandy, creme de cacao and cream into cocktail shaker.
  2. Add crushed ice and shake.
  3. Strain through sieve into glass.
  4. Sprinkle with nutmeg.

Spiced Ginger Mojito

Ingredients

  1. 2 x Rum
  2. 1/2 x Simple syrup
  3. Splash of Ginger ale
  4. 6 x Mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Muddle mint, simple syrup and 1/4 lime in cocktail shaker.
  2. Add spiced rum.
  3. Add ice and shake.
  4. Strain into glass filled with ice.
  5. Top with ginger ale.
  6. Garnish with lime and mint leaves.
